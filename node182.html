Error Bounds for Computed Eigenvectors.

Keep the assignments to \widetilde\lambda, \lambda, and \delta as above, and let x be the eigenvector of A corresponding to \lambda. The error bound for the computed eigenvector \widetilde x and the true eigenvector x are
\sin\theta_B(x,\widetilde x)\le\frac 1{\delta}\cdot\frac {\Vert r\Vert _{B^{-1}}}{\Vert\widetilde x\Vert _B}.
 (97)

This, (5.27), and (5.28) yield
\sin\theta(x,\widetilde x)\le\Vert B^{-1}\Vert _2\frac {\sqrt{2\kappa(B)}}{\delta}\Vert r\Vert _2.
 (98)

The factor \sqrt 2 can be removed by a more elaborate argument, but we shall omit it here.
